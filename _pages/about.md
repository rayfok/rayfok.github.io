Hi! I am a 2nd year PhD student at the Paul G. Allen School for Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by <a href="https://www.cs.washington.edu/people/faculty/weld">Dan Weld</a> and <a href="https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~jfogarty/">James Fogarty</a>. Broadly, my research explores the development of human-centered AI systems. My current projects are investigating AI-infused interfaces, specifically in applications of supporting reading scientific papers and accessibility.

I received my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Math in 2019 from the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor, where I worked with <a href="https://web.eecs.umich.edu/~wlasecki/">Walter Lasecki</a> on crowdsourcing methods and applications, and data-driven solutions for accessibility.

For more information check out my <a href="files/cv.pdf">CV</a>.
